FOSS targets STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering and Math) to make sure our students help shape the future.

## STEM Lab Facilitator

When the Slavens STEM Lab was built, FOSS stepped up to pay the salary of a full-time Lab Facilitator.

## STEM Lab 3D Printer

3D Printing is becoming commonplace, allowing people to create parts, toys, ideas quickly and cheaply.  In order to help Slaven's students learn and understand this technology, FOSS recently bought a 3D printer and supplies for the STEM lab.

## Hour of Code

The (link: https://hourofcode.com/us text:Hour of Code) is a national wide effort to get kids into writing software.  Slavens participates and FOSS helps out with the materials.
