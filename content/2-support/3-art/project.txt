FOSS helps with Art at Slavens help it grow and thrive.

## Art Supplies

FOSS makes sure the school's art programs have all the necessary materials.

## Art Framing

When student art needs framing, FOSS is there to make sure it happens.

## Fall Arts Festival

Our Fall Arts Festival is a chance for our students to show off their artistic creations.  FOSS helps with materials and food!
